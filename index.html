Stage points are highest-only — you bank the value of the furthest round reached, not a running total. Reach the quarters and that's 9; go all the way and it's 18 (not added on top of the earlier rounds).
Knockouts can't draw. A tie after extra time goes to a shootout: the winner takes the 3 points and advances, the loser gets nothing.
Clean sheets ignore the shootout — only goals conceded across the 120 minutes count, so a 0-0 that goes to penalties gives both teams a clean sheet.
Underdog bonus: +3 per win for the 12 lowest-ranked nations (South Africa, Qatar, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Haiti, Curaçao, New Zealand, Saudi Arabia, Cape Verde, Iraq, Jordan, Uzbekistan, Ghana) — an underdog win is worth 6.
End-of-tournament awards: the owner of the nation that wins the Golden Boot, Golden Glove, Best Player or scores the fewest goals gets +4 each; most cards in the group stage is +3. Added once the tournament finishes.
Every point traces back to a match on the Feed tab.
Timing: stage points are credited when a team's match at that round is played, not the moment they're drawn into it. So in the short gap between a round being drawn and played, a team won't yet show the next round's points — that's deliberate, so the Feed and the totals never disagree.
The third place playoff doesn't count for shit — idc if you moan.
In case of draws on points between us, the tiebreaker is GF, after that it is GD, after that we run one board on pokerrr2.
